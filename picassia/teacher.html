    FAQ

    • Who can publish a course on Domestika?

      Any professional in the creative field. A team of professionals is responsible for reviewing every proposal in detail to ensure that it meets Domestika's quality standards. In addition to your course proposal, it's essential that you send all the necessary information so that we can have a clear picture of who you are and what you do.

    • Will I have to pay?

      No, you wonâ€™t have to spend a cent. Domestika will take care of the course production costs and, on top of that, you'll earn money.

    • What do I need?

      A Domestika account and a burning desire to teach and share your knowledge with our creative community. We'll take care of the rest.

    • What kind of courses can be suggested?

      The courses should be aimed at creating a project in any of the Domestika areas: photography and video, illustration, 3D and animation, marketing and business, design, calligraphy and typography, architecture and spaces, craft, web and app design, music and audio, writing, and fashion, etc. Domestika's mission is to help millions of creatives learn new skills, advance their careers, and explore their hobbies while sharing their knowledge and projects with the community.

    • What are Domestika courses like?

      No two courses are the same. A Domestika course usually has between two and four hours of content, and we recommend for at least 60% of that to be in video format, but we'll advise you in detail on all these topics in due time.

    • What do I need in order to receive payments for my courses?

      All you need is a PayPal account. As soon as we verify it, we'll send you the instructions so that you can easily receive the payments.
